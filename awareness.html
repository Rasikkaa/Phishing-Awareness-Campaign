What is Phishing?

Phishing is a type of cyber attack where attackers impersonate legitimate organizations via email, text message, or websites to steal sensitive information such as usernames, passwords, and credit card details.

How to Spot a Phishing Attempt:

  • Check the sender's email address carefully.
  • Look for generic greetings instead of your name.
  • Beware of urgent or threatening language.
  • Hover over links to see where they really go.
  • Check for spelling and grammar mistakes.
  • Never enter credentials on suspicious sites.

Stay Safe Online!

If you suspect a phishing attempt, report it to your IT department immediately.
